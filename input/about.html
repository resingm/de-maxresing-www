About

My name is Max Resing, as you might have guessed. Since 2018, I have been studying Computer Science & Engineering at the University of Twente in Enschede, the Netherlands. Primarily, I am a Linux enthusiast and try to steer my career into cyber security. I believe in the potential of web services and the cloud. In my free time, I try to experiment with web services, to broaden my knowledge. Casually, I try to improve my knowledge in C to be able to contribute to some open-source projects.

In 2014, I started my official computer-science career as an apprentice at GWS mbH. After finishing my apprenticeship, I continued working there which results in a total of 4 years of work experience. The apprenticeship taught me to manage and to implement enterprise software. In summer 2015, I challenged myself during my full-time apprenticeship with an extra qualification of the Chamber of Industry and Commerce, called "European Assistant". This additional qualification realigned my personal orientation completely. An internship at AlphaCC in Bray, Ireland, gave me first insights on work experience abroad. Since then, I decided to move to Dublin for 6 months to gain proficient language skills. In the mean-time, I was working remotely. I finished my stay with an IELTS certificate.

So far, I have gained a great skill set which helps me to solve my daily challenges. I am proficient in Java, C# and Python. During my apprenticeship, I also gained decent C/AL know-how. HTML, CSS and JavaScript are familiar, but I would not consider myself to have it mastered yet. As already mentioned, I am a beginner in C and Rust. Besides those skills, I try to teach myself new things whenever I can.

For further questions, I would refer to my Contact page.
