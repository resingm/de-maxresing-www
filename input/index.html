Welcome!

My name is Max Resing. I am a full-time computer science student, part-time full-stack developer, enthusiast of open source software and a passionate system administrator of my humble home lab - a stack of Raspberry Pis dedicated to personal services and projects.

During my early childhood I developed my interest in computers. With the age of 8 years I got my first own computer. It got an Intel Pentium processer, 256MB memory, 700MB disk space and was powered by a glorious Windows 95 OS.

My unsatisfiably curiousity urged me to dig deeper and deeper into the world of computer science. I developed a mentality that computers can make anything possible. The limits are defined by our imagination and our will of investment.

These days, I am focussed on Linux environments and on teaching myself the Rust programming language. Occasionally, I try to challenge myself with cyber security challenges. As every enthusiastic computer science engineer, I love spending time on side projects, open source software and new technology. In my spare time, I also love to read and to travel.
